Style Guide 1.0

Purpose/Theme

Our theme is meant to be fun, playful, and fluffy and is centered around: EXERCISE KITTENS! The colors, patterns and images used in our theme are inspired by the exercise craze of the 1980's and 90's. We are drawing color and typography inspiration from classic VHS exercise tapes and are combining that with the cuteness and fun of kittens. Think Richard Simmons, Jane Fonda, sweatbands, hot pink, and leotards :)

Branding

The main branding of our theme revolves around our exercise cats who represent different fitness communities.
